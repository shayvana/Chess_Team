Running this app will take you to the login page. Type in a team name and password. The app will let you know if the team name and password already exist. If they do not, the app will
automatically register your team name and password in the database. After logging in, you may create a new match, which will take you to a page that allows you to keep score of your
boards. You may also view past conference results, which will take you to a web page displaying past results. I hope you find this app informational on the world of team chess, of which
IMSA was State Champion last year!
